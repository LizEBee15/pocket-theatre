Pocket Theatre

Building an inclusive community around theatre.

We designed an app that provides a platform for theater enthusiasts to find shows within their local communities, share their experiences & find the best deals for tickets.

As a theatre lover myself, I find it hard to keep up with whatâ€™s playing at the local theatre, and what broadway shows are coming to big cities near my location. Therefore I wanted to create an app that would offer information and tickets to all available shows, both big and small.

Timeline

3 weeks, Team Project

My Role

Experience Strategy, Research, Ideation, Prototyping

Deliverables

User Journey + Stories, Prototypes, Lo-Fidelity Mock-Ups, Process

PROBLEM

People often miss out on amazing shows playing in their area.

Local shows are not well-advertised, and theatre enthusiasts have to scour the internet and research for showings at theatres near them.

Theatres do include their upcoming seasons and schedules on their websites, but even the most avid theatre-goer has a hard time keeping up with what is playing where and when.
